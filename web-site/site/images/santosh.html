About TripLived

TripLived is an android application, which records and enriches a traveller's travel experience in real time. The application intelligently analyzes travel moments based on user activity, and makes them a part of the travel story. From in-trip suggestions to photo-album creation, automatic check-ins, and social connect, the application works as a travel assistant to make sure that every travel story becomes a lifetime memory, and is shared with friends and family to inspire them.
